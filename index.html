Meet our team!

Chef & Owner - Anthony Garcia

Possessed of humble beginnings as a home chef, Anthony transformed his love and passion for the cuisine of his peoples into a kitchen that proudly serves the greater Las Vegas community. As a second generation filipino American, food was the main way I felt I could connect to my heritage! I now share that love and enthusiasm with any and all who love filipino food, or are willing to give it a shot!
